ABOUT US

Ease the Error is the flagship event of forum of datascience engineers (fodse) club of Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering. It serves as a platform for the participants to showcase their talents and expertise in project development and deepen their problem-solving skills. After the successful events of Ease the Error 1.0 and 2.0 the Forum of Datascience Engineers (FODSE) & The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers Student Forum are set to conduct the “Ease the Error 3.0” as an inter-collegiate event. This event serves as an opportunity for the students to upskill themselves on recent technologies.
